How it's calculated (Simplified):

We project your account growth from your current age until 55. The calculation assumes:

  • Your salary grows annually by your specified percentage.
  • New savings are distributed into SA and OA based on standard rates.
  • At age 55, we first move your SA balance into the RA (up to the FRS limit).
  • If there's still a gap to reach the FRS, it is taken from your OA.
